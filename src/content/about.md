Hi! I'm brugr aka Kevin, I go by [they/he pronouns](https://pronouns.brugr.com) and I'm 20 years old.

I'm a huge tech nerd who's mostly interested in self-hosting with the intention to get into some kind of sysadmin role sometime in the future. Other tech interests are open-source, privacy and (not surprisingly) Linux, especially immutable distros.

The tech interest grew early on from video games, where I started with [Minecraft](https://www.minecraft.net/) and [Team Fortress 2](https://store.steampowered.com/app/440/Team_Fortress_2/), two games which I hold near and dear to my heart.  
Current favs are [Deep Rock Galactic](https://store.steampowered.com/app/548430/Deep_Rock_Galactic/), [Powerwash Simulator](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1290000/PowerWash_Simulator/) and a lot of the new "survivors-like" games, like [Brotato](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1942280/Brotato/), [Soulstone Survivors](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2066020/Soulstone_Survivors/) and [Vampire Survivors](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1794680/Vampire_Survivors/).  
I used to be a pretty heavy rhythm-game player as well, getting decently high on the [Beat Saber](https://store.steampowered.com/app/620980/Beat_Saber/) leaderboards, but after hurting my wrists I don't really play them anymore.

The types and genres of music I regularly listen to are [drum and  bass](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Drum_and_bass), "harder styles" like [hardstyle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hardstyle), [speedcore](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Speedcore), [rawstyle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hardstyle#Rawstyle), [frenchcore](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Frenchcore) and more. I've also recently started getting into [psytrance](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Psytrance).  
Some favorite artists (in no order) include [Camellia](https://musicbrainz.org/artist/151bb385-1af0-40b6-9269-bf2ed982311f), [Massive New Krew](https://musicbrainz.org/artist/9aed24f5-4293-4c5b-a7c9-1027c8d9d608), [Kobaryo](https://musicbrainz.org/artist/2ac4d851-c5d9-48f7-8164-699cc5ce02d1), [USAO](https://musicbrainz.org/artist/f734ee01-8c48-4541-8522-6f15600b4e45), [MUZZ](https://musicbrainz.org/artist/6cfdec30-4730-4513-bfb4-5db3ff566900), [Sub Focus](https://musicbrainz.org/artist/8cf49f40-b8fe-4a63-b4ea-f922d6145bb4) and more that I'm too lazy to list.
